# Survey Questionnaire

## Title

University Students' Perceptions of Using Generative AI in Academic English Writing: Usage, Benefits, and Plagiarism Concerns

---

## Instructions for Respondents

Thank you for participating in this research study. This questionnaire aims to understand your perceptions of using Generative AI (GenAI) tools — such as ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, etc. — in academic English writing.

- There are no right or wrong answers. Please answer based on your honest experience and opinions.
- Your responses are completely anonymous and confidential.
- Participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time.
- Estimated completion time: 10-15 minutes.
