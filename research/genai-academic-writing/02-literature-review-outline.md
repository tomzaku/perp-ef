### 3.2 Writing Process Enhancement
- Overcoming writer's block
- Generating ideas and outlines

### 3.3 Self-Directed Learning
- Immediate feedback without teacher dependency
- Personalized explanations of errors
- Exposure to native-like phrasing

## 7. Summary and Research Gap

- GenAI adoption is growing rapidly but perceptions are mixed
- Benefits for non-native English writers are promising but under-researched
- Plagiarism concerns remain unresolved, especially in non-Western contexts
- Vietnamese students represent an under-studied population
- This study addresses the gap by providing quantitative evidence from a Vietnamese university context
